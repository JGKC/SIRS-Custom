Our Cotton

Our Cotton is harvested from the sunny fields of USA where they are treated with the utmost care to produce only the highest quality cotton for your T-shirts.

Weaving Process

After harvesting, our cotton fibres is sent to a facility where they are turned into loose strands before being knitted on a looming machine.

Assembly

The cotton pieces are then delicately cut and stitched together in this stage to form your t-shirts.

Distribution

Once done, your favourite T-shirt is then shipped to the various distribution facilities to be shipped to the stores near you.
